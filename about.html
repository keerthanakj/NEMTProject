About Us

What is patient transportation service?

NEMT services are usually intended for medical appointments or other forms of non-emergent care. Non-emergency medical transportation (NEMT) includes transportation services offered to patients and healthcare consumers who face extraordinary barriers getting to their medical appointments. NEMT providers are required to offer transportation both to and from the appointment, utilize the most appropriate form of transportation, and include coverage for all expenses associated with transportation.



When can I use patient transportation service?

Nowadays children are very busy taking their older parents to their health checkups and it is difficult for the older parents to go alone to the hospital. So, they tend to not go to the hospital and skip their checkups. For disabled people, a caretaker is needed to look after them. It is difficult for them to travel alone in public transportation and they need a helper to be with them in the checkup. So, we are here providing patient transportation and including with that we are also providing caretaking service i.e, to go along with the patient and be there with them throughout the health checkup, after their checkup we take the responsibility to drop them to their respective destination.
