Our Service

Service For


Older Adults

Aged people who are above 45 and older are elegible for this service. Aged person who may drive less frequently or not driver at all. They may lack access to a personal vehicle, and therefore need to rely on family members or public transportation. Older adults may also experience physical challenges related to aging, such as visual and mobility impairments, that impact their ability to drive or use public transportation services.

Disables

People with disabilities also experience transportation barriers. In India out of 1.36 billion population, 21 million people are suffering from one or the other kind of disability. And in the case of rural areas, 69% of disables are present. People with disabilities living in rural communities often face challenges accessing and using various modes of transportation, which can impact health and quality of life.

Our Facilities



Booking online medical transportation

The Non-emergency medical transportation provide safe transportation. Transportation service can be booked earlier. Medical transportation service contain both to and from to the respective destination with complete care.


Basic Life support equipment

Transportation service will have basic medical equipment like wheelchairs, stretcher, oxygen cylinder, blood pressure monitoring equipment, pulse oximetry etc.


Patient Caretaker

Patient Caretaker exclusively provides Non-Emergency Medical Transportation (NEMT) services for both ambulatory and clients that use wheelchairs to and from their destinations with door to door aid when necessary. And for disables caretaker are very much necessary to look after them. Our goal is to make sure you arrive safe and on-time.
